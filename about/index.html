About

I was a Conservatory kid that started making music from an early age, and later in life became a composer for others by chance. My work has been featured by brands like Coca-Cola or New Balance, by Agencies like Mccann Erickson or Ogilvy, or in producer companies like Pedro Almodóvar’s «El Deseo». But you won’t find any music under my name out there. If you want to come with me in the process of changing that, you can hit some of the icons below. More on that on my manifest.
